Lata Mnageshkar

Born: 28 September, 1929

Role: Singer

Death:6 February 2022

Lata was born on September 28, 1929 in Indore and has worked extensively in various spheres of Indian popular and light Classical Music. She has sung Ghazals, Bhajans, and Pop. In fact, she has sung over 50,000 songs for three generations of heroines, from Madhubala to Zeenat Aman to Kajol.
